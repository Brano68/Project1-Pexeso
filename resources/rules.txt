At the beginning the player can choose if he wants to play against PC or another player.
Player will pick a pair of cards and if they match, player score will increase by 2.
Player with a higher score will win.
Four random cards are hidden Black Peters (&&) and if one is picked, player score wil decrease by 3.
By picking a pair of Black Peters, player score will decrease by 6.
To return to main menu, enter 1.
By entering a random key the game will quit.
